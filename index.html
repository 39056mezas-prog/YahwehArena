Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

For Indoor Open Ride, riders bring their own horse. If you don't have one, our Horsemanship Clinics pair you with one of our own well-trained horses — just mention it when you book.

Yes. A $200 deposit secures your date for Venue Rentals, with the remaining balance due one week before your event.

Deposits are non-refundable but fully transferable to a new date within 12 months if your plans change. Since the arena is fully enclosed, weather almost never affects a booking.

Leashed, well-behaved pets are welcome on the grounds. For the safety of horses and riders, pets aren't permitted on the arena floor during rides or events.

Riders must be at least 6 years old for Indoor Open Ride and clinics. Riders under 12 must be accompanied by a parent or guardian.

Closed-toe boots with a heel are required for all riders. We provide a limited number of helmets to borrow at no cost — reserve one when you book if you don't have your own.
