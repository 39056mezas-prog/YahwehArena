Riding & Clinics

Ride, Learn, and Explore the Trails

An 80' x 120' fully enclosed indoor arena on Yahweh Ranch means you ride every season — plus clinic hosting, an outdoor round pen, and trails right outside the gate.

Riding

Indoor Open Ride

$15 per horse & rider · $10 each additional horse

Ride comfortably through every Minnesota season on fresh, well-managed, low-dust footing in our 80' x 120' indoor arena — protected from snow, heat, and bugs alike. Open riding has no set time limit — come during open hours and ride at your own pace.

  • Open 7 days a week
  • Bring your own horse, or ride one of ours — we have 2 horses available to rent
  • Fresh, well-managed, low-dust ground
  • Heated warming house on site to warm up between rides

Outdoors

Trails & Round Pen
