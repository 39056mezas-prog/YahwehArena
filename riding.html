Riding & Clinics

Ride, Learn, and Explore the Trails

An 80' x 120' fully enclosed indoor arena on Yahweh Ranch means you ride every season — plus horsemanship clinics and trails right outside the gate.

Riding

Indoor Open Ride

$15 / rider

Ride comfortably through every Minnesota season in our spacious, well-ventilated 80' x 120' indoor arena — protected from snow, heat, and bugs alike.

  • Open 7 days a week
  • Excellent ventilation, year-round comfort
  • Accommodates approximately 10 horses
  • Bring your own horse and tack
Instruction

Horsemanship Clinics

$175 / rider

Hands-on clinics built to grow real confidence and skill in the saddle, led by instructors who care about doing it right. Don't have a horse? We'll pair you with one of ours.

  • Three sessions, four riders per session
  • Professional instruction & riding exercises
  • Lunch and drink bar included
  • Small groups available on request
Outdoors

Trail Riding

Horse trails wind through the countryside surrounding Yahweh Ranch. Bring your own horse and explore at your own pace, or ask us about guided options when you book.
