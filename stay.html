Farm Stay

A Peaceful Getaway on the Farm

Camp right on Yahweh Ranch with a full RV electrical hookup, surrounded by horses, a friendly mini donkey, and the quiet of the Minnesota countryside. Campsite and RV accommodations are ready now.

Life on the Farm

Real Farm Life, Right Outside Your Door

Living on the property: four horses, a Great Pyrenees, two miniature Australian Shepherds, and one very friendly miniature donkey who has the run of the place. There's also a flock of chickens — help yourself to fresh eggs, right out of the coop.

The whole property is fenced with a gated entrance, so it's safe and easy for kids and pets alike. Bring your own horse to ride, or ride one of ours — we have 2 horses available for guests.

Traveling with your own horse? Summer retreat lodging includes a private pasture and stall for your horse, with short or extended stays available.

Your Campsite

Camp Right by the Arena

Four 30-amp RV electrical hookup sites sit right around the indoor arena, with water on-site and a heated warming house nearby — ride, relax, and fall asleep to the quiet of the country. Campsite and RV accommodations are ready and open now.
