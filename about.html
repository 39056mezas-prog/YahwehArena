Meet the Owner

Meet Jodi

I'm a Certified Nurse Practitioner by trade — my expertise has always been in saving lives, not horses. Two years ago I bought a small 20-acre hobby farm here near Long Prairie, and I recently finished building an 80' x 120' indoor arena with four RV electrical hookups.

It started six years ago when I bought my daughter her first horse, then one for myself. Today we have four horses between us, my daughter is in nursing school, and this little hobby farm has grown into something I never expected: a place I get to share with families and individuals looking to slow down and experience real farm life.

Yahweh Arena is the featured facility here on Yahweh Ranch — our 20-acre family farm, home to our horses, a Great Pyrenees, two miniature Australian Shepherds, a very friendly miniature donkey who has the run of the property, and a flock of chickens.
